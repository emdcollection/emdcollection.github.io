The Benefit/s of Meditation

Meditaion can provide many benefits to someone. Two of these are Controlling anxiety and Reducing stress. These two are the main reasons one would perform meditation, as they are trying to release themselves from the stress, or mellow themselves out so that they can approach their problems much easier. Two more beneifts are enhancing self-awareness and increasing attention span. These are obviously great benefits, as they increase skills that greatly aid your life. The final benefit worth mentioning is how it affects your mood. Meditation has the potential to make you a nicer person, being happier to yourself and to others, even if this emotional improvement is only minor.
