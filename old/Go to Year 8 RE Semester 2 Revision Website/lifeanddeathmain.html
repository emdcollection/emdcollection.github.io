RES2R: Life and Death

Key Terms

Some important key terms relating to Life and Death can be found below.

Death: The end of someone's mortal life on earth. Different cultures have different meanings of death, but it generally refers to the end of the main segment of one's life.

Afterlife: What occurs after death. Usually, Afterlife has three different states; Heaven, Hell, and Purgatory. Different interpretations of the afterlife can be found between different religions.

Soul: The spiritual and immortal part of a human being.

Purgatory: A temporary place of punishment. In Christianity, people in purgatory are punished for their sins before going to Heaven. Purgatory is usually the middle ground between a Good and Bad afterlife.

Reincarnation: The rebirth of the soul into another body.

Heaven: Eternal Paradise. An immortal life in the greatest paradise empty of sin.

Hell: Eternal Punishment. Dpicted as an immortal life filled with never-ending suffering.

Sheol: The Hebrew underworld (The Hebrew Purgatory, where souls wait to be judged to see if they shall enter heaven).

Resurrection: Returning to the mortal world in your body. In Christianity, it specifically refers to Jesus Christ coming back to life.
