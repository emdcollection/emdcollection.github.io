Social Justice The Thin Green Line

What is The Thin Green Line?

The Thin Green Line Foundation is a foundation aiming to provide park rangers the resources they need, as they believe Park Rangers are undersupported. They've provided resources to Park Rangers all over the world, and even support the families of fallen rangers. Originally, "The Thin Green Line" was a documentary which greatly succeded, and after said success, the foundaton was made. In the documentary, most of the services that the foundation provide today were performed.



How does this show Social Justice?

This is a great example of social justice. Although mainly focusing on Park Rangers, the service provides aid to a group of people who have been publicly considered undersupported. This is what social justice means. It could also be an example of "preferation of the poor," where less fortunate people who risk their lives to make money are eing supported more than a different job in no need for such support.
