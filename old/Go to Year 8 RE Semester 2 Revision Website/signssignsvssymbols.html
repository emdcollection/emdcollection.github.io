S.S.S Signs VS Symbols

What is a Sign and Symbol?

A sign is a notice or object that points the way and gives names, such as shop names, road signs; A uniform can also be a sign, for example, a fire officer, or school uniform.

A symbol is an object which carries a special meaning for a person or a community such as a wedding ring or a school badge. Symbols can have many layrs of meaning and they can help people in expressing and understanding some of the most important aspects of humanity and spirituality. They can be an action like a handshake or an object which carries religious meaning.



Sign VS Symbol

A sign is different to a symbol because it bears very little religious importance. Signs are also used as a practical aid in life, while symbols usually relate to religious or much more important matters.
